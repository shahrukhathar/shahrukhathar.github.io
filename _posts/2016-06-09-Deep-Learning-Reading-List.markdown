Here are a bunch of resources for learning about Deep Learning and its applications.

# Courses and Video Lectures
1. [Andrew Ng's Introduction to Machine Learning](https://www.coursera.org/learn/machine-learning)
2. [Geoffrey Hinton's Neural Networks for Machine Learning](https://www.coursera.org/course/neuralnets)
3. [Hugo Larochelle's Neural Networks class](https://www.youtube.com/playlist?list=PL6Xpj9I5qXYEcOhn7TqghAJ6NAPrNmUBH)
4. [Yann LeCun's Lectures at College de France](http://www.college-de-france.fr/site/en-yann-lecun/course-2016-02-12-14h30.htm)
5. [Quoc Le's Lectures on Deep Learning](http://www.trivedigaurav.com/blog/quoc-les-lectures-on-deep-learning/)
6. [Yaser Abu-Mostafa's Learning from Data](https://work.caltech.edu/telecourse.html)
7. [Machine Learning at Oxford](https://www.cs.ox.ac.uk/people/nando.defreitas/machinelearning/)
8. [Vision and Learning at MIT](http://web.mit.edu/course/other/i2course/www/vision_and_learning_fall_2013.html)
9. [CS231 at Stanford](http://cs231n.github.io/),its [video lectures](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=2uiulzZxmGg&list=PLrZmhn8sSgye6ijhLzIIXiU9GNaIwbF8B) and [aaditional material](http://vision.stanford.edu/teaching/cs231n/syllabus.html)
10. [NVIDIA's lectures on Deep Learning](https://developer.nvidia.com/deep-learning-courses)
11. [UC Berkeley's course on Deep Reinfrocement Learning](http://rll.berkeley.edu/deeprlcourse/)
12. [Machine Learning at CMU](http://www.cs.cmu.edu/~tom/10701_sp11/lectures.shtml)
